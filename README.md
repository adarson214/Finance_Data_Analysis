# Abigail-Finance-Data-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)
## Project Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze personal finance transactions for 2025 to gain insights into spending habits, income sources, and savings patterns. This will help in making informed decisions, optimizing expenses, and increasing savings for future financial goals.

## Questions (KPIs)
- Compare Income vs Expenses
- Which month had the highest expenses and outcome?
- Which category contributes the most exepenses?
- What are the top 3 expenses subcategories?
- Relationship between spending categories and payment method.
- Percentage between spending categories.
- Which month has the highest spending?

- Dashboard Interaction <a href= "https://github.com/adarson214/Abigail-s_Portfolio/blob/main/Screenshot-BudgetCard-2025.png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Data Cleaning: Checked for missing values, duplicates, and inconsistent formats; corrected where necessary.
- Categorization: Standardized expense categories and labeled income, savings, and credit transactions.
- Pivot Tables: Created pivot tables to summarize monthly income, expenses, savings, and payment types.
- Visualization: Combined pivot tables into a dashboard using charts, slicers, and tables for interactive exploration.

## Project Insight
- Income comes primarily from salary and passive income, while expenses are highest in Food, Entertainment, and Gas.
- April and December had the highest spending due to seasonal purchases.
- Top 3 expense categories: zelle, Groceries, and Entertainment, contributing ~55% of total expenses.
- Digital payments (Zelle) are used more frequently than cash.
- Monthly savings rate is ~8% of income.

  
## Final Conclusion:
To optimize personal finances in 2026, focus on controlling discretionary spending in Food, Entertainment, and Gas, especially during weekends and high-spending months. Increasing automatic savings and tracking digital transactions can further boost financial stability. Regular monitoring using this dashboard will support informed budgeting and spending decisions.
